Project Summary: Latitude and Weather

In this project, I examined weather data from cities around the world to discover how various weather attricubutes change with lattitude. The weather attributes I looked at were cloudiness, humidity, wind speeds, and temperature. Data was gathered from the OpenWeather API. After data was gathered, I used Matplotlib to construct several deifferent plots including comparisons between latitude and temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity. The website constructed includes the aforementiones plots as well as a data page that displays our Data.
