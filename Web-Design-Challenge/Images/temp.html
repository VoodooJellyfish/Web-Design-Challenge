Latitude vs. Max Temperature

When you examine the relationship between Latitude and Max Temperature, a pattern can be observed where it seems that the closer to a Latitude of zero that you move, the warmer your city will be. In general, it also seems that temperature and North you move, whereas the correlation seems weaker as you move South.
