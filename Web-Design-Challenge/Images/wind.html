Latitude vs. Wind Speed

The data seems to show that wind speed seems to increase the further away from zero in latitude you travel. This is especially true the further North you move, but is also true to a weaker extent the further south you move. If you are looking for a calm-winded city, it would make the most sense to make sure you move somewhere close to the equator
